Our Trust Principles

We organize our trust commitments across five dimensions:

🔍 Product-Level Trust

  • Transparency: Every decision is explainable with clear audit trails
  • Reliability: Enterprise-grade uptime and performance SLAs
  • Compliance: Aligned with SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, HIPAA
  • User Control: You own your data — export, review, or delete anytime

📋 Process-Level Trust

  • Validation: Independent audits and reproducible benchmarks
  • Risk Management: Explicit risk modeling and mitigation
  • Customer Engagement: Transparent roadmaps and feedback loops
